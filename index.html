Build web apps beautifully and quickly

batman.js is a full-stack microframework extracted from real use and designed to maximize developer and desiner happiness. It favors convention over configuration, template-less views, and high performance by simply not doing very much. It all adds up to blazingly fast web apps with a great development process; it's batman.js.
